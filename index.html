Getting Started

You start in Sanctuary, a safe town. Walk up to the glowing NPCs and pinch to interact:

  • Waystone — Travel to unlocked dungeons.
  • Captain — Pick up bounty quests for gold & XP rewards.
  • Trader — Buy and sell equipment.
  • Keeper — Stash items you want to keep safe.

Dungeons & Progression

Use the Waystone in town to enter a dungeon biome. Each biome has 4 floors of increasing difficulty, ending in a boss fight on floor 4.

Kill the boss to unlock the next biome. Biomes in order:

  1. The Crypts — Skeletons, ghouls, wraiths. Boss: Lich Lord.
  2. Overgrown Ruins — Spiders, thornlings, wisps. Boss: Old Druid.
  3. Frozen Peaks — Frostwolves, frost giants, ice bats. Boss: Ice Wyrm.
  4. Infernal Depths — Imps, hellhounds, demons. Boss: Archdemon.
  5. The Void Spire — Voidlings, nullweavers, crystal sentinels. Boss: Void Lord.

Each floor has a portal to advance deeper, and a portal to return to Sanctuary at any time.

Combat

Your hero auto-attacks the nearest enemy in range — just walk close. Each class has a different attack style (melee cleave, arrows, magic bolts, or bone wands).

Pinch to use your active skill (costs mana, has a cooldown). Skills are powerful AOE or multi-hit abilities.

If you die, you respawn in Sanctuary with a small gold penalty. Your items and progress are safe.

Loot & Equipment

Enemies drop items of varying rarity:

  • Common — No bonus stats.
  • Magic — 1 bonus affix.
  • Rare — 2-3 bonus affixes.
  • Legendary — 4 affixes; named items change your look.
  • Mythic — 5 affixes, 2-3 sockets, prismatic aura.

Open the menu → Inventory to equip gear (weapon, armor, ring, amulet). Better gear means more damage, defense, and crit chance.

Leveling Up

Kill enemies to earn XP. Each level grants 3 stat points to spend on:

  • STR — Increases damage.
  • INT — Increases damage for mages & summoners, plus mana pool.
  • DEX — Increases crit chance and a bit of damage.
  • VIT — Increases max HP.

Spend stat points from the menu → Character screen.

Classes

  • Warrior — Melee cleave attacks. Skill: Whirlwind (huge AOE spin).
  • Mage — Fires arcane bolts at range. Skill: Frost Nova (freeze + damage).
  • Ranger — Shoots arrows rapidly. Skill: Multishot (5 arrows at once).
  • Summoner — Bone wand at range. Skill: Raise Dead (summon skeleton minions).

Tips

  • Stock up on potions from the Trader before entering dungeons.
  • Pick up bounty quests from the Captain for bonus gold & XP.
  • Dash (↑↑↑) to dodge boss attacks or escape when low on HP.
  • Stash your best backup gear with the Keeper so you don't lose it.
  • Rare and Unique items glow on the ground — don't miss them!
